Mars — Key Facts
Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun in the Solar System.
Average distance from the Sun: about 227.9 million km (1.52 AU).
Diameter: approximately 6,779 km.
Surface gravity: about 3.71 m/s² (0.38 g).
Two small moons: Phobos and Deimos.
Evidence for past liquid water includes valley networks and hydrated minerals; present-day surface conditions do not support stable liquid water.
Sources: NASA planetary data and peer-reviewed studies.
